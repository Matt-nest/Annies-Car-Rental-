The Overview

What you're getting

Your platform is three connected pieces that run your rental business end-to-end — from the moment a customer lands on your site to the day the car comes back.

1

Your branded booking website

A fast, mobile-first site on your own web address where customers browse your fleet, pick dates, e-sign the rental agreement, and pay by card — with a built-in live chat widget.

2

Your owner dashboard ("Fleet Command")

A private admin login to approve bookings, manage your fleet and availability, track revenue, handle pickups/returns & damage, and issue refunds — all in real time.

3

Automated payments & messaging

Secure card payments through Stripe, plus automatic email & text confirmations, pickup/return reminders, and owner alerts — no manual follow-up required.

How this guide works

Each step below is tagged so you always know who's doing what. You means we need something from you. Our team means we handle it. Work through the "What we need from you" list first — that's what unblocks everything else.

Phase 1 · Your Checklist

What we need from you

Gather these items and send them over in one batch. The sooner we have them, the sooner you go live. Tick each box as you complete it.

You  You provide / decide Our team  We set it up

Business identity & branding

Business name, logo (PNG/SVG), brand color, contact phone & email, physical/service address, and operating hours.

You

Your web address (domain)

The domain you want customers to visit (e.g. yourrental.com). If you don't own one yet, we'll help you register it.

You

Your fleet list

For each vehicle: year, make, model, photos, daily rate, deposit, mileage limits, and any add-ons. A simple spreadsheet is perfect — we'll load it for you.

You

Rental terms & policies

Minimum age, deposit & cancellation rules, insurance requirements, mileage/fuel policy, and late-return fees. We turn these into your Terms of Service and Privacy Policy pages.

You

Stripe payment account

Create (or connect) a Stripe account so payouts go straight to your bank. ~15 min You'll need your business & bank details handy. We connect it to the site.

You

Sending email address

The "from" address for booking confirmations (e.g. bookings@yourrental.com) and the owner email that should receive new-booking alerts.

You

Reviews & photos optional

Customer testimonials and a few hero/lifestyle photos make the site convert better. Send what you have — we'll polish the rest.

Phase 2 · Our Build

What our team sets up

Once we have your Phase 1 items, here's what happens behind the scenes. No action needed from you — this is for transparency.

Provision your private database

We spin up your own secure Supabase project for bookings, customers, fleet, and payments — isolated to your business.

Our team

Brand & deploy your booking site

We apply your logo, colors, fleet, and policies, then deploy the customer site and your owner dashboard to fast global hosting.

Our team

Connect payments end-to-end

We link your Stripe account, wire up secure card capture and deposit holds, and configure payment webhooks so every transaction is tracked.

Our team

Set up email & text automation

We verify your sending domain and connect the messaging engine so confirmations, reminders, and owner alerts fire automatically at every booking stage.

Our team

Connect your domain & secure it (SSL)

We point your domain to the site, issue HTTPS certificates, and set up the admin subdomain for your dashboard.

Our team

Add live chat & create your login

We install the live-chat widget and create your secure owner account so you can sign in to the dashboard on day one.

Our team

One thing we may ask you for

To connect your domain and verify your email, we'll send you 3–5 small DNS records to add at your domain registrar (or you can grant us temporary access and we'll do it). It takes a few minutes and is the only "technical" step on your side.
