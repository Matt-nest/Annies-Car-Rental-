Questions, answered straight

Do you check my credit?
Nope. No credit check and no big deposit to get started. If you've got a valid license and you're ready to drive, we can work with you.
Is insurance really included?
Yes — commercial-grade coverage is built into your weekly rate, along with maintenance and roadside. No surprise add-ons.
Are the miles really unlimited?
Unlimited, every week. Gig work means high mileage, so we never charge per mile. Drive as much as you need to.
How fast can I start?
Most drivers are approved and rolling the same day. We can do a same-day pickup or deliver the car right to your door on the Treasure Coast.
Which apps can I drive for?
Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Uber Eats, Instacart, and Amazon Flex. Rideshare or delivery — you're approved to drive on all of them.
What do I need to qualify?
A valid driver's license and an active gig account (or one you're about to set up). Message us and we'll walk you through it in a couple of minutes.
Where do you serve?
Port St. Lucie and across the Treasure Coast — Fort Pierce, Stuart, Jensen Beach, Vero Beach and Palm City. We'll deliver to you.
Can I cancel?
It's a weekly flat rate — keep the car as long as you're earning, hand it back when you're done. No long contract.
