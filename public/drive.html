FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much is the weekly rental?
Weekly rentals start at $279/week for eligible vehicles. We'll confirm the final price and terms before you book.
Is $279 the total price?
We'll confirm the full payment amount before you book, including any deposit, taxes, fees, delivery charges, mileage terms, insurance details, or other rental requirements.
Do I need to apply or just check availability?
Apply first. The application helps us confirm the best vehicle, timing, requirements, and pickup or delivery options.
How fast can I get a car?
Same-day pickup may be available depending on vehicle availability, your documents, payment, and location.
Do you deliver the car?
Delivery may be available in Port St. Lucie and nearby Treasure Coast cities. Ask about your location when you apply.
Is insurance included?
We'll explain the insurance and coverage details before you book. Make sure you understand what is included, what is not included, and what you are responsible for before taking the vehicle.
Is there a deposit?
Deposit requirements may vary. We'll confirm the deposit requirement, if any, before you book.
Are there mileage limits?
Mileage terms will be confirmed before booking. If you plan to drive heavy mileage, tell us during the application.
Can I use the car for DoorDash, Uber Eats, Instacart, Amazon Flex, or courier work?
Many drivers use rental vehicles for delivery, courier, grocery, package, or service-app work. Requirements vary by platform, vehicle, and driver account. Tell us what you plan to use the car for and we'll help you review the fit.
Can I use the car for Uber or Lyft?
Do not assume every rental vehicle can be used for Uber or Lyft. Some platforms have specific rental and vehicle rules. Tell us which platform you plan to use, and we'll help you confirm whether an available vehicle may fit the current requirements.
What do I need to qualify?
You should have a valid driver's license, valid phone number, payment method, and meet Annie's rental requirements. Additional documents may be requested before approval.
Can I rent longer than one week?
Weekly rentals may be extended depending on availability, payment status, and rental terms.
What happens if the car needs service?
Contact Annie's right away. We'll explain the next step based on the vehicle issue and your rental agreement.
