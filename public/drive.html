FAQ

Questions, answered straight

How does the 50% off first week work?
Your first week is half price when you start a 3-month rental commitment. It's our way of helping you get on the road and earning while you ramp up. We'll confirm your exact first-week rate, deposit, and terms in writing before you book, nothing hidden.
Do you really not run a credit check?
Correct, approval is based on your driver profile, documents, and our standard rental requirements, not your credit score. Bad or no credit does not automatically disqualify you.
Is insurance really included?
Yes, coverage is built into your weekly rate. We'll walk you through exactly what's covered, what's not, and what you're responsible for before you take the vehicle.
Are there mileage limits?
No. Every rental comes with unlimited miles, so you can drive as much as you need for gig work with no mileage caps and no per-mile overage charges.
How much is the deposit?
A fully refundable deposit of $350–400 is collected at pickup to secure the vehicle. It's returned after you bring the car back in good condition, minus any incidental charges like tolls or unpaid balances. You'll have the exact amount confirmed in writing before you book.
How fast can I get a car?
Often same-day, it comes down to what's available, your documents, and payment. Apply and we'll give you a straight answer.
Can I use the car for Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart or Amazon Flex?
Many drivers use our rentals for delivery, courier, grocery and rideshare work. Requirements vary by platform, vehicle, and your driver account, tell us what you plan to drive for and we'll help confirm the fit before you commit.
Do you deliver the car?
Yes, pickup or delivery across Port St. Lucie and nearby Treasure Coast cities. Tell us where you are when you apply and we'll confirm.
What do I need to qualify?
A valid driver's license, a valid phone number, proof you're 21+, and a payment method. Some documents may be requested before approval, we'll tell you exactly what's needed.
Can I rent longer than a week?
Yes, most weekly rentals extend. Keep your payment current and let us know before your week ends.
